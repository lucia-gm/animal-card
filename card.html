West Highland Terrier

It will not tolerate rough handling such as a child pulling on its ears, and can be both food- and toy-possessive.

  • Origin: Scotland
  • Color: White
  • Average wheight: 15-20 lb
  • Average lifespan: 12 to 17 years

Commonly known as the Westie is a breed of dog from Scotland with a distinctive white harsh coat with a somewhat soft white undercoat. They have bright, deep-set, almond-shaped eyes that are dark in colour. Their ears are pointed and erect. It is a hardy breed, and can be stubborn, leading to issues with training.
